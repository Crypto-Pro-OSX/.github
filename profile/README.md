## 🖌 Overview  
Crypto Pro Advanced is a comprehensive cryptocurrency portfolio tracking application that gives you real-time visibility into your holdings across all major coins and exchanges. Live prices, charts, and market data are always current and always at hand.

Portfolio tracking covers your full position — purchase price, current value, profit and loss, percentage gain, and allocation breakdown. Every metric you need to understand your portfolio at a glance is presented clearly on a single screen.

Price alerts fire instantly when any coin crosses a threshold you set, letting you stay informed about significant market movements without having to check the app constantly.

Detailed chart views cover multiple timeframes with technical indicators, volume data, and market cap information that supports more informed decision-making about your positions.

For crypto investors who want professional-grade tracking and market insight without the complexity of a full trading platform, Crypto Pro Advanced delivers everything needed in a clean, focused application.
